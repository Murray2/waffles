Waffles
(or Pancakes)

Curricular Outcomes:

  • Waffles
  • Pancakes
  • Chiffon Mixing Method
  • Chantilly Cream

Knife Skills

  • Strawberries

Ingredients:

  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Cups AP Flour
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla

  • 1/2 cup whip (heavy) cream
  • 1 tbsp icing sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
    •

Procedure:

Ok, so my butter was in the freezer. Here is a trick from industry to get your butter out of the package when frozen:

Preheat waffle iron. Microwave the butter to room temperature. Beat eggs whites in a large bowl with 1/2 the sugar until fluffy, they should be soft peaks not stiff. Tip: Make sure your bowl is perfectly clean or your egg whites will not whip up.

Put the egg whites aside in a bowl and beat the yolks with the remaining sugar. The yolks should be pale yellow and fluffy. Prepare the rest of your mise en place during mixing.

Mix the remaining wet ingredients into the dry ingredients (omit the second set of ingredients: cream, icing sugar, and vanilla).

Fold in your whipped eggs.

Cook according to your waffle irons instructions. If you do not have a waffle iron, cook some pancakes with this batter.

Slice strawberry fans, in halves with green on, or quarters without green stem. What ever you prefer.

Chantilly Whip Cream: whip the ingredients in the second part of the ingredient list to soft cream peaks.
